The RF domain focuses on wireless communication, enabling data transmission, signal reception, and antenna design for various applications, including CubeSats and HAM radio activities.

Antenna Design – Designing and optimizing antennas (dipole, Yagi, patch, helical) for satellite communication, telemetry, and tracking.
